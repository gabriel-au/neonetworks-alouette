fit elephant

 

Fit Elephant is a fun, physical program that helps kids develop into strong, healthy, confident children with a love of life and a passion for their world. Central to the program are the concepts of fun, friends and fitness. This means that each and every lesson is devoted to the development of friendships through fun activities that build core motor skills. The most important part being that each and every child is having fun! The program is designed to assist children in developing important physical skills, as well as environmental, nutritional and safety awareness. Fit Elephant focuses on developing fine and gross motor skills, to assist children to develop core physical skills, while also building fitness. This introduces children to a love of physical activity and the outdoors, aiming to ensure that children continue this interest into their schooling years.

 

Fit Elephant is designed to cater for the necessary developmental stages of children, preparing them for their schooling years, in a fun fitness focused environment. The program consists of numerous fun and challenging activities, incorporating the use of balls, ropes, cones, hoops and balloon games. This full body workout will have the kids crawling, ducking, weaving and jumping all to our fun jungle animal moves. Our fantastic weekly lessons lay the foundation to all sporting activities developing fine and gross motor skills and a range of spatial skills concepts which are key to school readiness.

Fit Elephant makes a real difference to children’s lives, encouraging friendships and love of fitness, as well as teaching skills and topics such as:

 

Jungle Fitness - keeping kids strong, fit and happy

Anatomy - our bodies are amazing

Environment - what a wonderful world

 

Each lesson includes activities and conversations regarding these instrumental topics, preparing children for their upcoming schooling years in a safe, fun environment. By developing these skills and the children’s knowledge base, Fit Elephant ensures that children are truly learning and enjoying their time.

 

Jungle Fitness

Remember Tarzan? He was strong, fit and ready for anything. Fit Elephant lessons develop jungle fitness through a range of animal walks, crawls and jumps that are fantastic fun and really challenging. Through our games and activities, children develop:

Coordination, balance, strength, speed, agility, fitness, self-confidence,
assertiveness and determination.

 

Anatomy

Our bodies are amazing! We teach children to love and respect their bodies, from top to toe and inside out. Our instructors lead simple, interactive discussions on a variety of topics including:

Our Body, Our Senses, Our Skeleton, Our Muscles, Our Digestive System, Our Circulation System, Our Respiratory System, Our Nervous System and Our Immune System

 

The Environment

What a wonderful world we live in! The Fit Elephant program opens kids’ eyes about our amazing planet, including fascinating facts about endangered animals and simple ways for each and every one of us to make the world a better place for the future. Our instructors lead simple, fun discussions on topics such as:

About the Planet, Habitats, Endangered Animals and Ways You Can Help

 

* Fit Elephant is delivered by Childhood Australia through their Wild Things Program.

The benefits include:

• helps children sleep
