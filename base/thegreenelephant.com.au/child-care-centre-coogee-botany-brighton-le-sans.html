BRAND NEW CHILD CARE CENTRE IN COOGEE, BOTANY AND THE BRIGHTON LE SANDS AREA

 

Studies show that the more days children spent in daycare, the better they were able to use communication styles (http://www.sciencedaily.com). That's good news for working parents who typically spend about 8-10 hours away from home and their kids each day. Savvy parents in the Coogee, Botany and Brighton Le Sands communities count on the Green Elephant Child Care Centres to provide quality child care five days a week for their children.

 

Since October of 2015, we have been offering a groundbreaking all-inclusive child care centre serving kids from birth to school age. We provide everything for your child's day from nappies and formula to nutritious homemade meals cooked on site by our experienced chef. Offering little extras such as a morning and afternoon tea time, we strive to create an atmosphere rich with warmth just like at home. Also, our play-centred curriculum is comprehensive and we are dedicated to providing an excellent education for your child from birth to school age.

 

Working Parents Affirm the Green Elephant Child Care Centre in Brighton Le Sands

 

We know that you have a lot to manage as a working parent so we make things easier by providing nappies as well as formula for our younger children. We also make it easy to pay your child care fees by using an auto system that utilises the latest technology called 'DebitPro'. This secure system directly debits your fees from your account after you provide your account details. It's that easy and having payments automatically deducted from your account means one less thing on your to-do list!

 

Not only is our centre easy to get to (both of our offices are easily accessible by public transport) but we cater for children from birth to school age. That means we can provide quality child care services to many children, including the communities of Coogee, Botany, Brighton Le Sands and the surrounding areas.

 

Child Care Centre in Coogee offers All-Inclusive Curriculum

 

We offer a unique all-inclusive balanced curriculum to enrich and nourish every child. In addition to a specialised outdoor programme, our qualified staff encourage kids to reach their highest learning potential by using age appropriate technology. For example, the older children attending our child care centre in Brighton Le Sans utilise technology such as iPads and computers.

 

There is a designated art space and we offer an unparalleled art education that encourages children to understand their world and learn through creating. Our gardening and sustainability programmes, as well as our comprehensive music programme, helps to develop children's musical abilities and skills. Innovative fitness and wellbeing programmes teach children to love physical activity develop motor and coordination skills and stresses the importance of being healthy.
