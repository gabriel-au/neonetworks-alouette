PLAY-CENTRED LEARNING - WHERE TO FIND A DAY CARE CENTRE IN MASCOT, RANDWICK, MOORE PARK AND MAROUBRA

 

Studies have shown that children who are allowed to learn through play will develop the intellectual, communication and social skills that are crucial for aptitude later in life. Why Children Need Play | Scholastic.com). For example, play has been linked to higher adjustment levels in school, greater reading aptitude, higher overall literacy and an increased potential for greater academic success and confidence.

 

The Green Elephant Child Care Centre provides day care centre services in Mascot and the surrounding areas with a play-centred learning philosophy. Our aim is to create a safe, caring environment and foster learning through play. As we have discovered a holistic approach to learning, we offer a diverse curriculum to encourage and celebrate every child.

 

Day Care Centre in Randwick Provides Personalised, Holistic Child Care

 

Our dedicated educators work closely with your child, encouraging the development of skills learning via an engaging curriculum and a variety of programmes that focus on a child's needs, interests and growth. We offer eight 'elephant' programmes: the Fit Elephant, Safe Elephant, Groovy Elephant, Hi-Tech Elephant, Tongue-Tied Elephant, Creative Elephant, Healthy Elephant and the Big School Elephant. Each of these is specifically designed with your child's interests in mind, whether it be music, technology, movement or a little of each!

 

For example, the Fit Elephant offers a curriculum that is fun and physical while the Groovy Elephant harnesses the power of music and includes lessons involving musical instruments and dance. Kids grooving in the music room are taught by music therapists and get to practice their listening, communication and reasoning skills while gaining the confidence to move creatively. There's also the hi-tech programme, which empowers kids to be safe and responsible technology users.

 

While some day care centres serving residents in Maroubra and the surrounding areas of Mascot, Randwick and Moore Park may offer day care, none offer the diverse curriculum that we do. For example, the Tongue Tied Elephant teaches second languages through story-telling, language and games.

Day Care Centre Services for Working Parents in Moore Park

 

The Great Elephant Child Care Centre accommodates about 60+ children a day in our two facilities. Our services are easily accessible by public transport and we cater to children aged from birth to school age.

 

Whether you live in Mascot, Randwick, Moore Park or the Maroubra area we can provide quality child care services for your family. Our varied curriculum strives for excellence in early childhood education and offers child care, long day care services, preschool and traditional day care. From our outside play area with a dedicated outdoor instructor to our music centred therapy programme, we have something for every child. Our dedicated instructors also teach life lessons and water safety.
