What is Critical Period Plasticity?

 

As any early learning centre in Zetland would attest, critical period plasticity is the time (typically between the ages of two and six) when the brain is at its most active. Developing neurons prove sensitive to information, and the foundations of key social, physical and educational moments are created. Children rapidly absorb information.

 

The Green Elephant, as the leading early learning centre in Alexandria and beyond, understands the importance of this period - and our curriculum is carefully tailored to take advantage of it, providing children with a stimulating environment.
