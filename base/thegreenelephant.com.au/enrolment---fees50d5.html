We are now accepting enrolments for children 0-6 years of age for 2016.
To secure a placement for your child, you will need to follow the step by step guide below.

Please note that The Green Elephant has a minimum two day enrolment requirement.

 

1. Fill out the Enrolment Enquiry Form below.

 

2. On receipt of your completed form you will be contacted by a member of our team.
   We will advise you if your chosen days are available and send you an enrolment package.
