about us

Welcome to The Green Elephant Early Learning Centre.

 

We are a family owned and operated centre and having three young children of our own, understand the importance of providing an environment where children feel safe and secure much like home.

 

We realise the level of trust you place in us when enrolling your child. Our promise to you is that we are committed to providing the highest quality standard of care and education possible.

 

Family is paramount to us and working with families and young children is what we love to do. Creating the best opportunities for children, where they are encouraged to explore, learn and develop in a loving and nurturing environment is our goal.

 

Each and every child at The Green Elephant is important to us and we welcome you to join our family. We look forward to meeting you and your child soon.

 

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At The Green Elephant our philosophy is to provide the highest level of care and education for your child in a warm, nurturing and safe environment.
By creating a feeling of acceptance and sense of belonging, we provide an environment which enables each child to feel safe and secure.

 

The Green Elephant provides educationally appropriate and stimulating programs to assist in the development of the whole child, their physical, social, emotional, cognitive, creative and moral growth. Indoor and Outdoor experiences are provided in small and large group situations.
These experiences cater for each child’s individual development and reflect current interests of the group or an individual child. We recognize the value of play on development. Research has found that children attain higher levels of achievement through engaging in play.

 

“Play provides opportunities for children to learn, as they discover, create, improvise and imagine.
Children's immersion in their play illustrates how play enables them to simply enjoy being.”

 

-- EARLY YEARS LEANING FRAMEWORK, 2009 --

 

 

Our educational play-based programs assist each child to build and develop at their own individual rate with guidance and support by our passionate educators:

     • confidence, curiosity and imagination

     • independence and self-esteem

     • respect for themselves and others

     • friendly and caring behaviour

     • their full potential both emotionally and
        physically

     • foster and reinforce self control

     • an understanding and appreciation of the
       rights and feelings of other people

 

Our programs also reflect the full appreciation of Australia’s multicultural society, enhancing the children’s awareness of, and respect for, cultural differences and similarities. Our programs are designed to help your child in developing all areas for success now and in later years and to equip them with the necessary tools to deal with life.

 

The Green Elephant endeavors to create a
“family-like” atmosphere and recognise the importance of creating a link between home and the Centre, where parents, educators and the community work in partnership to develop the child as a whole in a mutually supportive and caring environment.
