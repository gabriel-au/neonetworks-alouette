ALL INCLUSIVE CENTRE

 

The Green Elephant believes that no child should go without. We have adopted a holistic approach to education.  We are an all inclusive centre where all children participate in our 8 key Elephant programs.

 

These  include:

• Fit Elephant

• Creative Elephant

• Tongue-Tied Elephant      • Hi-Tech Elephant

• Healthy Elephant

• Groovy Elephant

• Safe Elephant           
• Big School Elephant

 

All the children are provided with home made meals tailored by our nutritionists and cooked by our friendly chef. Formula, nappies
and sunscreen are also provided.
