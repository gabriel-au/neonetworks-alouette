The Green Elephant is a Centre where your children can build skyscrapers and bridges,

rockets and friendships, confidence and creativity. Children are provided everyday with the

opportunity to move mountains, discover Picasso, travel the world, cook up a storm, think,

laugh, and most importantly discover who they want to be.

We are a family owned and operated centre and having three young children of our own, understand the importance of providing an environment where children feel safe and secure much like home.
