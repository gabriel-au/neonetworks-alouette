Alouette Child Care believes in providing a boutique and homely environment for families and children.

Our goal is to provide children with opportunities  where they are able to learn and socialise amongst their peers as they take part in the provided educational programs.

 

 

Alouette Child Care was established in 2009 when we purchased two low occupancy centres in Crows Nest and Eastlakes, Alouette worked on refurbishing the center ,updating the  programing, staffing and relationships with families and local neighbourhood which saw the centres return to full occupancy. Alouette has recently purchased our third centre in Chifley in 2013 .

 

 

Alouette Child Care believes in allowing our educators to spend as much time as possible with the children in their care and as such we employ a dedicated administration team to look after all enquiries , booking information & centre fees for each of our three child care centres. 

 

Our centres also have the support of our office team who have over twenty five years experience in the child care industry and can assist the centre managers are required each day of the week.
